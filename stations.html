Stations

The Red Team Village has several stations (mini hands-on workshops) at DEF CON 30 with numerous exercises where attendees can practice their skills and learn new ones. Most stations run for a period of one (1) hour. You will have different opportunities to participate in multiple stations at the Red Team Village during DEF CON 30. All the stations will take place at the Flamingo Mesquite Room (3rd floor).
