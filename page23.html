Hacking Meta Quest

By: Paul D.

Abstract:

Meta is teaming up with HackerOne to sponsor the Red Team Village at DEF CON 31! In this workshop, we'll provide Meta Quest 2 devices and training to dive into hardware hacking. No need to try to extract firmware, we have made it available to support static analysis and other firmware-based security research. Please see our payout guidelines, how to submit reports, and review our responsible disclosure terms for more information.
