Presented by Omar Santos

WebSploit Labs is a learning environment created by Omar Santos for different Cybersecurity Ethical Hacking (Web Penetration Testing) training sessions. WebSploit Labs includes tons of intentionally vulnerable applications running in Docker containers on top of Kali Linux or Parrot Security OS. It also includes several tools that do not come with Kali or Parrot OS and over 9,000 cybersecurity resources. Between all the vulnerable applications and additional challenges, WebSploit comes with over 450 distinct exercises!

In this station, you will get access to all these resources and "homework" that will definitely help enhance your cybersecurity career (whether you are preparing for a cybersecurity certification or just passionate about ethical hacking, bug hunting, and red teaming).
