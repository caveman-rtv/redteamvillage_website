HackerOps: DevOps for Conducting Security Assessments

Friday, August 12, 2022 13:00 - 17:00 PDT
Saturday, August 13, 2022 10:00 - 17:00 PDT

Presented by Ralph May from Black Hills Information Security

This training will help students apply DevOps for conducting security assessments. The goal is to teach the fundamentals of infrastructure as code to speed up deployment times on red teams.

In this station, you will:

- Gain a thorough understanding of advanced attack infrastructure
- Learn a full methodology for building red team resources
- Get access to instructions and sample code on how to build out full attack infrastructure and how to integrate TTPs.
